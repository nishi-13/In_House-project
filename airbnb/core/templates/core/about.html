About us


In-House is a marketplace platform that connects people who need a place to stay with people who need to rent out extra space. By offering an easy-to-use platform and flexible booking options, In-House has become one of the most popular ways to find accommodations around the country.

In-House solves the problem of finding a place to stay when travelling, whether you're looking for a budget-friendly option or something more luxurious. With millions of listings in hundreds of cities, there's sure to be a perfect match for your needs. And if you're looking to rent out your own space, In-House makes it easy to list your property and get started earning extra income.
